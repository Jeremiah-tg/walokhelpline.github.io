Become A Volunteer

Crisis has no borders. There are millions of people struggling all over the world and millions in Uganda. And we want to help all of them. Everyone deserves free, 24/7, mental health support at their fingertips. WALOK is here to fill the GAP

Walok-helpline is powered by volunteer Crisis Counselors who work remotely — anywhere with a computer and secure internet connection works.

We accept applications for volunteers in the region. If you’d like to become a volunteer from any part of the country, and want to volunteer, please complete the form below and we will contact you when the application is ready.

Crisis Counselors commit to 100 total hours of volunteering, serving a recommended 2 hours per week to meet this requirement. Before you begin as a Crisis Counselor, you’ll also complete a training period to give you all of the expertise you need to successfully and empathetically navigate crisis intervention.

If you’re interested in volunteering, please fill out this form and someone from your team will be in touch shortly.
